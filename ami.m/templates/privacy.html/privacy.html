Your account is protected by a password. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential. We recommend using a strong password and not sharing it with anyone.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@amimovies.com.

Our Services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information.
